People of openSUSE

Interviews with the people who make openSUSE

The "People of openSUSE" is an interview series with the people who form the community of openSUSE, those people who spend their time and effort to make all the great things happen in openSUSE. The series ran from 2007 to 2011. In 2016 we are rebooting it and are adding interviews with more people. Stay tuned and learn to know who makes openSUSE.

    See the page about how to do interviews with the People of openSUSE for some instructions and guide lines for doing interviews.

    The people we ask are people who are involved in the openSUSE community in some way. We are trying to have a diverse selection of people with different background and doing different type of work.

    The sources for this page can be found in the people git repo of the openSUSE organization. Feel free to open issues or submit pull request there for any suggestions you might have.
